Hello!

I'm a third-year Doctoral student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California, Davis. I'm doing research on the theoretical analysis of robustness of Reinforcement Learning under supervision of Prof. Lifeng Lai.

During Master's program, I conducted research on Internet of Things and Smart Home at Columbia Intelligent and Connected Systems Lab under supervision of Prof. Xiaofan (Fred) Jiang.

I also worked as a Network Engineer Intern at the start-up WiLO Networks, where I was responsible for smart farm prototype development and testing.
